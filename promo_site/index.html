SpeedRun

Let's go outside!

You're almost out of time! Run towards the heart orbs and collect them to increase your time limit. Gather them all and advance to the next round. Explore streets that you never even knew existed. Rank up to show off just how huge your calves are. Try to get through the most rounds and share the results with your friends! Remember, don't get hit by a car or be stupid.
